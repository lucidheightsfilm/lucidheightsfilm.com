The Vision

Film is not
captured.

I'm Cody — a filmmaker based in Portland, Oregon. I believe the most powerful stories aren't told, they're felt. Every project I take on begins with a single question: what do you want people to feel when they watch this?

From the quiet tears at a first look, to the commanding presence of a brand that owns its market — I craft films that don't just document moments, they make them immortal.

How We Work

From first call
to final frame.

I

Discovery Call

We start by understanding your vision, your story, and what success looks like. No templates. No scripts. Just a real conversation.

II

Creative Direction

We develop a cinematic concept tailored to your project — from shot lists to music direction to the emotional arc of your story.

III

Production Day

On the day, we move like ghosts — present for every moment, invisible in every photo. You live it. We capture it.

Film Delivery

A private screening link arrives in your inbox. Your story, color-graded and scored, ready to share with the world — or keep forever.
